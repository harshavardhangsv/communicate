Tagging Complex Sentences

To avoid ambiguity on a complex sentence, user can give the marking of each simple sentence present in the complex sentence
Ex: for a sentence,
राम ने कहा कि वह घर जाएगा |
we will have provide the interface,

The simple sentences here are: राम ने कहा and वह घर जाएगा
To tag the sentences, please click on the starting word and ending word of a sentence respectively for each sentence.
